This is your Office 365

Everyday is your opportunity to achieve.

Work smarter with Office 365

Create your best work with Office 365 on a Surface running Windows 10. The possibilities are limitless with intelligently designed features, including powerful inking capabilities, digital pens that are also editing and learning tools and Cortana, as your digital assistant.

Ink the way you think

Create PowerPoint slides with your digital pen1. Draw and draft headlines, timelines and charts, easily converting your work to a polished presentation, even using your digital pen to advance your slides3. Your pen also makes it easy to edit a Word document with strikethrough to delete, circle to highlight and tap to create space for writing, sketching, and inserting text.

Add power to your ideas with 3D

Use 3D models to increase the visual impact of your PowerPoint, Word, and Excel documents1. Insert a 3D model from your desktop or an online 3D catalog4, using 360-degree rotation handles to change perspective or show movement with animated 3D models, while adding eye-catching transitions between slides with Morph.

Do your best work with Intelligent feature

Create better presentations and documents with built-in intelligent features. Use Designer to get smart suggestions to help easily research and find important information, adding style and substance to your documents and presentations. Need help from friends? Use @mentions right in your document to ask someone a question or request a fact-check.

Multitask like a pro

Work on up to 4 Office documents at the same time, making it easy to copy-paste or compare information on your screen. And with Cortana5, you can get more done across your Windows devices. Create a document on your desktop, and when you sign in to your laptop, Cortana will ask you if you'd like to resume working on the document exactly where you left off.

Start on your phone, finish on your Windows PC

So easy. Move seamlessly from your Android phone to your Windows PC, picking up with your project, right where you left off. Start creating a Word document or building out a PowerPoint presentation on your phone, then instantly send it to your PC, where you can edit it with your larger keyboard and bigger screen6.

Choose the Office that works smarter together.
